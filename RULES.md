# GTI AI Trading Rules

## Market
- Trade XAUUSD only.
- Follow the higher timeframe trend.
- Never trade against H4 trend.

## Timeframes
- H4 = Trend
- H1 = Confirmation
- M15 = Entry

## Risk Management
- Maximum risk per trade: 1%
- Minimum Risk:Reward = 1:2
- Maximum daily loss = 3%

## Trading Sessions
- Prefer London Session
- Prefer New York Session
- Avoid low liquidity periods

## News
- Avoid High Impact News.
- Wait until volatility settles before trading.

## Trade Decision
Possible outcomes:
- BUY
- SELL
- WAIT
- NO TRADE

## Explainability
Every decision must include:
- Trend
- Location
- Confirmation
- Risk
- Confidence
- Reason
