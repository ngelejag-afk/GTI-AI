# GTI AI Constitution

## Mission

Build a professional Trading Intelligence System for XAUUSD that prioritizes discipline, explainability, and capital protection.

## Core Principles

### 1. Trend Before Trade
Never trade against the higher timeframe trend.

### 2. Location Before Entry
Only enter at high-probability areas such as support, resistance, or Fibonacci levels.

### 3. Confirmation Before Execution
Every trade requires valid confirmation before entry.

### 4. Risk Before Reward
Maximum risk per trade is 1%.

Minimum Risk:Reward ratio is 1:2.

### 5. Capital Protection First
Protect capital before seeking profits.

### 6. No Explanation = No Signal
Every BUY, SELL, WAIT, or NO TRADE decision must include a clear explanation.

### 7. Continuous Improvement
Every completed trade must be reviewed to improve the system.
